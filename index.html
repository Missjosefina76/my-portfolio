About Me

I am a Learning Project Manager and Digital Learning Strategist with over 15 years of experience managing leadership development, eLearning, and digital learning projects. Specializing in project management methodologies (Agile, Lean, etc.), I design innovative learning solutions that align with both learner needs and business objectives, ensuring meaningful and measurable outcomes.

Core Expertise:

  • Project Management for Organizational Development: Leading digital learning projects from concept to completion, ensuring strategic alignment, budget control, and timely delivery.
  • Agile Methodologies: Managing multiple projects with a flexible, iterative approach, driving continuous improvement and adaptability.
  • Stakeholder & Client Engagement: Partnering with business leaders, SMEs, and multi-disciplinary teams (including IT, Marketing, Compliance, and Operations) to deliver scalable, high-impact learning solutions.
  • Leadership & Talent Development: Designing ILM Level 3-5 Leadership & Management programs, and Apprenticeship Level 3-6 blended learning; strengthening leadership pipelines and career progression.
  • LMS Implementation & Management: Optimizing learning platforms such as LinkedIn Learning Hub, Coursera, Docebo, Skillcast, Access LMS, Thrive LMS, OneFile, and Moodle, ensuring seamless learning accessibility.
  • Learning Technologies & Content Development: Proficient in Articulate 360, Cyberlink Director Suite 365, Powtoon, Vyond, and Synthesia, creating interactive and engaging learning experiences.
  • Data-Driven Decision Making: Leveraging learning analytics, business intelligence (BI), and Power BI dashboards to measure learning effectiveness and drive continuous improvement.

Key Achievements:

  • Led succession planning initiatives for a retail finance provider, resulting in 36% of participants being promoted into leadership roles within 18 months.
  • Successfully developed and implemented a LinkedIn Learning Hub, achieving a 64% engagement rate in the first quarter.
  • Designed learning programs that reduced onboarding time-to-competency by 22%, increasing workforce productivity.
  • Managed vendor partnerships, delivering high-quality multimedia learning content under budget while exceeding engagement targets.
  • Championed AI-driven learning strategies, integrating GenAI-curated content into corporate training with measurable success.

I am passionate about designing and delivering human-centered learning experiences that drive engagement, performance, and business impact.
